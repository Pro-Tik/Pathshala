Pathshala SaaS

Revolutionizing Coaching Center Management in Bangladesh.

Status: Planning Launch Sept 2026

Market Fit

Current Pain Points

  • Manual attendance leads to proxy entries.
  • 30% revenue leakage in uncollected fees.
  • Zero real-time communication with parents.
  • Paper records are easily lost or damaged.

The Pathshala Edge

  • Digital Check-in (App / Biometric).
  • Automated SMS reminders for fee dues.
  • Instant PWA alerts for student arrivals.
  • Secure cloud storage with daily backups.

Core Features (v1.0 Launch)

Smart Attendance

Robust Digital Check-in system via App or optional Biometric integration.

Fee Management

Automated invoicing, digital receipts (PDF), and payment status tracking.

PWA Support
Progressive Web App for desktop & mobile. No app store download needed.

Upcoming Expansions (v1.1)

Grading System

Input marks to generate instant rank lists and automated student result sheets.

Parent Portal

Dedicated dashboard for parents to view attendance, results, and payment history.

Digital Notice Board

Broadcast holidays, exam schedules, and center updates directly to parents' phones.
